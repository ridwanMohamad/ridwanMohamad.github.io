About Coldspace

Who we are

Coldspace is an integrated cold chain company, aiming to provide seamless logistics solutions that include storage & distribution of products across the value chains and provision of best-in-class value added services in between.

Our Vision

To be the leading integrated end-to-end cold chain solution with focus on technology and operational excellence.

Our Mission

To create a complete end-to-end supply chain ecosystem primarily focusing on cold chain and fast- moving consumer goods.

To empower businesses with agile, tech-driven top notch cold chain solutions.

To be the partners of choice in driving growth and expansion.

To drive sustainability agenda by focusing on mitigating food loss in Indonesia.

 

We are on-demand, warehouse marketplace that connects the supply and demand of warehouse spaces.
